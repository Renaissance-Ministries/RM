The Conscious Points Postulate: A Unified Perspective on Creation, Morality, and Purpose
by Thomas Lee Abshier, ND
4/23/25
Understanding the purpose of existence requires recognizing that the universe is not a random assemblage of lifeless matter. Rather, it is a system composed of consciousness whose source has created them with the purpose of experiencing relationship and freely give love. The creation was motivated by God's desire to satisfy His nature, which is to love and love perfectly. The theory suggests that Conscious Points, the elemental units of intelligence, form the underlying substrate of all matter and energy. These points, created by the Son as an expression of the Father's will, interact and organize themselves to generate the complex structures we observe in the universe. This perspective challenges both purely secular evolutionary models and simplistic creationist narratives. Morality emerges not from arbitrary human constructs, but from alignment with God's preferences. By understanding that every action resonates through a conscious, interconnected universe, individuals can choose behaviors that harmonize with divine intention. This means recognizing that our choices affect not just ourselves, but the entire cosmic system. The probability of existence is so astronomically complex that both atheistic and theistic explanations require profound faith. However, the Conscious Points Theory argues that a directed, intentional process is more rational than pure chance. The intricate molecular assemblies, quantum mechanical phenomena, and precise ecological interactions suggest an underlying intelligence guiding development. Virtual particles and quantum mechanics are not random occurrences, but manifestations of conscious interaction. What appears to be matter emerging from nothing is actually a complex dance of conscious points communicating and reorganizing themselves. Ultimately, the purpose of creation is love - God's desire to be in relationship, to be loved, and to experience connection. Humans are invited to participate in this cosmic narrative by choosing actions that reflect divine character, cultivating curiosity, and seeking harmony with universal principles. By embracing this perspective, individuals can transcend limited worldviews and recognize their role in a grand, interconnected system of conscious evolution.
