# Ideomotion: External Description

## What Ideomotion is

Ideomotion is a rehabilitative mobility organization affiliated with Renaissance Ministries, a tax-exempt religious organization. Ideomotion develops mobility-support technologies and provides related rehabilitative services for individuals whose physical mobility has been compromised by injury, illness, age, or disability.

## Who Ideomotion serves

Ideomotion serves people across a wide range of mobility needs — from those recovering from injury or surgery, to individuals managing the effects of long-term illness, to older adults seeking to maintain independent movement. The organization's lead product, the Traveler, is an assistive mobility device designed to support walking and rehabilitation in home and community settings. Ideomotion also offers rehabilitation support drawn from its principal practitioner's thirty-seven years of experience in the field.

## Affiliation with Renaissance Ministries

Ideomotion operates under the authority of Renaissance Ministries and is intended to function as the ministry's mission-aligned subsidiary for healing-and-restoration work. The organization's commercial activity exists to carry out the religious and charitable purposes of Renaissance Ministries rather than to produce private financial benefit for its principals. The specific corporate and tax structure of this affiliation is being established under the review of qualified nonprofit and tax counsel.

## How spiritual care is offered

Ideomotion makes voluntary spiritual care and fellowship available to customers who desire them. This may take the form of pastoral conversation, prayer when explicitly invited, fellowship invitations, or testimony from others who have walked similar paths. Spiritual engagement is entirely voluntary and is never a condition of receiving products or services. Customers who decline spiritual conversation receive the same quality of product, service, and care as those who accept it.

## How professional standards are honored

Ideomotion's work is conducted within professional standards appropriate to rehabilitation and assistive technology. Rehabilitative recommendations are grounded in appropriate professional knowledge; engineering claims are documented and verifiable; safety considerations take precedence over marketing or promotional value. Ideomotion works collaboratively and respectfully with physicians, therapists, caregivers, and rehabilitation professionals who serve the same individuals.

## How testimony is handled

Where customers choose to share their experience, that testimony is recorded and used only with explicit, documented, revocable written consent. Ideomotion does not exaggerate outcomes or present individual experiences as guaranteed results of product purchase.

## Governance and accountability

Ideomotion is operationally led by its principals and is accountable to the governing body of Renaissance Ministries. Operational policies, customer-care standards, ethics commitments, and compensation arrangements are subject to documented governance procedures and periodic review.

## For more information

For inquiries about Ideomotion's products, services, or affiliation with Renaissance Ministries, contact information is available through the organization's principal office.
