## §2. Claude's pre-essay analysis

Claude read the CFE operating system and the most recent essays (260530 set) to match frontmatter, voice, and the affirm-then-refine structure. The strategic frame Claude adopted, without needing to surface options because the prompt was directive:

- **Posture:** Address Peter directly, by name, as the June 1 essay addressed Leonard. Mirror the handling of the Mormon divergence from that essay — lay both lines side by side, give the fellowship's reasons, leave the discernment to the reader — rather than refuting.
- **Theological map of Peter's six moves and the calibration each needed:**
  1. *Derivative power* (John 19:11; Deuteronomy 32 / Psalm 82 divine-council reading of Babel; demons' terror) — **affirm strongly**; it is the strongest part of the letter and well-grounded in the text.
  2. *"Test drive" Christology* — affirm the solidarity intuition (John 14:9) but **correct toward Chalcedon** against two ditches: modalism (it is the *Son*, not the Father, who is incarnate) and Apollinarianism (a *complete* human nature assumed, not a body driven).
  3. *How God can die* — affirm "lingering materialism" as half the answer; **supply the other half** via the two-natures union and the *communicatio idiomatum* (Acts 20:28).
  4. *"Law of Spiritual Conservation of Energy"* — affirm the intuition; **anchor it** to aseity (John 5:26) and God's sustaining act; **flag** the "multiple lifetimes" clause against Hebrews 9:27, gently.
  5. *Hell as self-segregation* — affirm (Lewis, doors locked on the inside); **round out** with the active-judgment imagery.
  6. *Power of the word* (footnote) — affirm (Proverbs 18:21; James 3; Matthew 15:11); **distinguish** from name-it-and-claim-it; hold *pharmakeia* lightly.
- **Spelling-correction judgment:** Claude corrected obvious typos and, beyond pure spelling, repaired one genealogical slip (Peter's "Isaac became Israel" → the Abraham/Isaac/Jacob lineage with Jacob renamed Israel), flagging the change so Thomas could revert it. Claude did not reproduce the Bob Dylan lyric, keeping only the song-title allusion.
- **Placement:** CFE primary; CAP secondary (cultural/personal-correspondence engagement). Status FIRST_DRAFT pending Thomas's review.
